We offer both direct and indirect referral bonuses to clients who refer new clients to our services.

When you refer a friend, family member, or colleague to our company and they invest, you'll receive a direct referral bonus of 8% of the amount they invested. But the benefits don't stop there. If the investor refers someone else let's say Chris, you'll receive an indirect referral bonus of 3% of the amount invested by Chris.
